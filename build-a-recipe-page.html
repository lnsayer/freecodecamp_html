Lovely Daal Recipe

This recipe makes a delicious, creamy daal, which will warm your belly and soul. Suitable for vegans and gluten free alike.

Ingredients

  • 300g Red Split Lentils, washed
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala

Instructions

  1. Fry the onions until translucent then add the garlic, wait until the garlic starts to smell
  2. Add the lentils along with 400ml of water
  3. Cook the lentils until the water evaporates and the lentils form a liquid the consistency of golden syrup
  4. Add the garam masala, mix and serve hot
